Our Mission
WHY
WE
EXIST

We believe that live experiences — whether a football derby, a comedy show, a perfect dinner, or a rooftop party — are the moments that define our lives. Yet for too long, accessing these experiences has been fragmented, frustrating, and unreliable.

TicketZone was created to fix that. We've built the simplest, most trustworthy platform for Egyptians to discover, book, and attend the events they love — all in one place, with zero hassle.

From the Cairo Derby to an intimate restaurant reservation, every ticket we sell is a gateway to a memory. We take that responsibility seriously, and we're proud to serve over 50,000 fans, diners, and party-goers across Egypt.
